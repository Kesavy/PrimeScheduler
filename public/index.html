Questions

Do I need a Trello paid plan?
No. PrimeScheduler works on all Trello plans including Free.
Who posts the comment?
The comment is posted under your own Trello account — exactly as if you typed it yourself, just at the time you chose.
How far ahead can I schedule?
Up to 7 days ahead. Extended scheduling is coming in a future update.
Can I cancel a scheduled comment?
Yes, any time before it sends. Just open the card, click the badge and hit × next to the comment.
Is my Trello token stored securely?
Yes. Your OAuth token is encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored on EU servers. You can revoke access at any time from within the Power-Up.
